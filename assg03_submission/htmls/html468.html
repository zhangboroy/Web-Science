Dinamo Zagreb (a) Confirmed Line Up: Ospina; Debuchy; Gibbs & Arteta start

  • Print
  • Email
Details
Mean Lean
16 September 2015
Created: 16 September 2015

 

Arsène Wenger has shuffled his pack a little bit as the Gunners prepare to face a Dinamo Zagreb in hot form, with the Chelsea game looming around the corner, Arsène has been forced into making a few changes to the team that dismantled Stoke City on the weekend. Arsène Wenger had already shown his intentions before the game by leaving Hector Bellerin and Aaron Ramsey at home.

The biggest surprise is probably the choice of keeper. David Ospina who was first choice for a large chunk of last season will come in, in place of Petr Cech. Mikel Arteta also starts his first game of the season in place of Francis Coquelin.

Here is the team for today.

Confirmed Line Up:

Ospina, Debuchy, Gabriel, Koscielny, Gibbs, Arteta, Cazorla, Özil, Oxlade-Chamberlain, Alexis, Giroud

Subs: Cech, Chambers, Monreal, Flamini, , Coquelin, Campbell, Walcott

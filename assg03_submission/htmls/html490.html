Description

Gain an advantage over your Fantasy Football rivals.
Player and Team Stats provided by OPTA to help you pick your Fantasy League Team.

If you play fantasy football, this is the app for you!!!
FFStatsHQ displays player, team and match statistics provided by OPTA to help you pick the perfect fantasy football team. The app and access to statistics is completely free.

Wondering which midfielder is creating the most chances?
Wondering which defender is getting the furthest forward or putting in the most crosses?
Wondering which striker has had the most shots from inside the box?
Wondering which team has had conceded the most shots on target?
FFStatsHQ has the answers.

Our player and team comparison page allows you to directly compare up to 5 teams or 5 players over all the important statistics, grouped into categories such as 'Goal Potential' and 'Assist Potential' etc.

Our player and team statistics page allows you to find the top players and teams across any of these categories.
You can also review the stats for an individual player or team, game by game.

Articles written by our team provide in depth analysis of current trends to point you in the direction of the players with the most potential to score points.

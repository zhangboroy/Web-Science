Chelsea join Arsenal and Liverpool in January race for £7.5m Spain star

CHELSEA have joined the race to land Real Betis youngster Dani Ceballos.

By Rhys Turrell / Published 15th September 2015

The midfielder is a reported target for Arsenal and Liverpool.

And Tuttomercato cite reports in Spain which claim Chelsea are also chasing the 19-year-old.

A January move is on the cards for Ceballos, who is out of contract at the end of the season.

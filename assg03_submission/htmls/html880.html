Arsenal injury scare as Mohamed Elneny picks up calf problem while on Africa Cup of Nations duty

The Gunners will have to wait for more news from Egypt officials as the seriousness of the problem has not been confirmed

  •  Comments

Arsenal have been dealt an injury scare after Mohamed Elneny picked up a calf problem while on international duty.

The midfielder is away with Egypt at the Africa Cup of Nations and has been ruled out of the quarter-final clash with Morocco on Sunday evening.

Elneny's injury was confirmed by Egypt officials but offered no further information on his condition.

Arsene Wenger will be eagerly awaiting news with it unclear how long he will be out of action.

Arsenal will get Elneny back during the week if his country bow out of the competition on Sunday night.

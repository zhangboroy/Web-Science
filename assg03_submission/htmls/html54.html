Arsenal - the Invincibles

£70.00

'the invincibles' visually records Arsenal’s wonderful unbeaten season in 2003-04. Details of every game are displayed, including team lie ups and goalscorers.

Amy Lawrence, author of Invincibles, stated "Ooh, that's lovely."

Each image is a high quality giclée print, on a luxurious heavy weight Hahnemühle fine art paper.

The print sizes is available as a 50cm x50cm or 40cm x 40cm.

Prints will be sent in a strong postal tube. In the UK, we use the Post Office Special Delivery service. As each print is made individually, we aim to dispatch within five working days. We can deliver throughout Europe, the USA and other wonderful parts of the world.

Please note that the print is unframed. It is available as a framed print in the UK. And as each image is individually printed, every print is unique.

On the transfer window: “Very quiet. On our front we have a big squad. We are not in the need to buy.

“On the general front it is very quiet. All the teams in England, especially the big teams, have big teams.

“There is no news about Carl Jenkinson. Still open to move but it doesn’t look like something will happen.”

Back to Dat Guy welbz: “I’m pleased with performances individually.

“We had many players scoring goals but Danny was not there. Now we have huge offensive potentials.

“I have to use strikers well now without destroying balance of team.

“Perez has also contributed well. Offensively we have many players who can produce and score.”

Welbz: “Of course he has done enough to keep his place but we have to be cautious.

“We can’t use him every three days I think he is a bit short on that but the quality is there.

“His return – and the quality of all our strikers – offensively we have a strong squad.”

Have Watford improved: “I don’t know. We’ll see tomorrow night.

“We have created a run and it’s important we retain that.

“Our attitude is spot on and we are ready to fight tomorrow.”

smorgan 30th January 20179:24 am

Arsenal well-equipped to play three at the back? “Yes we can, when needed we can do that.

“But we are focused on the offensive and we like to go forward and have numbers middle of park and up front.”

smorgan 30th January 20179:25 am

Link to Benzema – why? “Because he’s French. There are some noises he would leave Madrid.

“In the summer window? That’s not my worry at the moment. What’s my worry is the next game against Watford.”

smorgan 30th January 20179:26 am

Wenger confirms Carl Jenkinson would leave on a permanent deal, if he goes.

Wenger said: “There is no problem with his attitude. He must find happiness with conditions and where he goes.”

No offers for Debuchy – still possible he could go. Wenger confirms he is fit and back in training.

smorgan 30th January 20179:30 am

And that is that from Mr Wenger.

There we go, then. It would appear things are going to be taken gently regarding Danny Welbeck’s full-time return to the squad.

Meanwhile it should be a quiet transfer day for Arsenal tomorrow – but there could be fireworks in the summer with a possible move for Karim Benzema.

There are no firm offers for Carl Jenkinson yet but any deal will be permanent.

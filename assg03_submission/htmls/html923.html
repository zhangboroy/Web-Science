Job Description

We are looking to hire an Mgr. Info Systems Eng. immediately. This is a great opportunity to join a rapidly expanding and successful team. We are looking for someone with a hands-on technical background who has effective communication skills. It’s your chance to work with the best in the industry in a solutions focused environment.

Pay Rate: $69.44 (Pay can be negotiated based on qualifications).

  • IT Planning and Solutions Service Line Manager. IT Planning and Solutions Service Line Manager (SLM) will be the owner, leader and internal advocate for the Planning and Solutions Line of business.
  • The SLM is responsible for ensuring the quality of the end to end services, service line strategy, portfolio management and roadmaps for the service line.
  • The IT Planning and Solutions SLM will capture and integrate IT/Business requirements from the Agency’s two/three letter organizations and the Service Operations Managers to deliver a forward looking (quarterly and 18 month) Service Portfolio view.
  • He/she will work as a partner with the appropriate Service Operations Manager to develop strategies necessary to optimize products and services in the line of business.
  • SLMs are highly skilled IT professionals whose technical expertise will be engaged in understanding the business needs of stakeholders across the Agency and translating them and assembling an appropriate service response.
Key Responsibilities include:
  • Working with senior stakeholders to define appropriate service levels and establish service line targets.
  • Set service delivery goals, overall priorities and success measures.
  • Responsible for the full lifecycle of products and services across the service line.
  • Leading the service line strategy and contributing to the overall Agency strategy and vision.

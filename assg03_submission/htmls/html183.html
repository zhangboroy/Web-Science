Arsenal to battle Manchester United for La Liga star


Written by Harneet Singh

No Comments  

Arsenal have joined the race for Atletico Madrid’s Antoine Griezmann, according to the Daily Mail. 

Surprisingly, the Gunners are ready to trigger the Frenchman’s Â£85m release clause and make him their record signing, but there’s a problem as Manchester United, Chelsea and Manchester City are all believed to be in the hunt.

At the moment, Manchester United stand as the clear favourites to land Griezmann, Paul Pogba being one of the reasons as to why that is the case. Also, Jose Mourinho is said to be ready to pay whatever wages the 25-year-old wants.

Whether or not does the deal go through depends on Atletico Madrid and if they want to cash in on their young superstar, because presently, Antoine Griezmann is happy to be where he is.

Arsenal 17-18 Home, Away And Third Kits Info Leaked

Sunday, January 29, 2017Sunday, January 29, 2017
Made for the already fourth time by Puma, the new Arsenal 2017-2018 jerseys will again update all previous designs as the Gunners will receive new home, away and third kits. Set to be released from late May / early June 2017, the new Arsenal FC 17-18 home, away and third jerseys are set to introduce unique designs for the North London club.

Arsenal 17-18 Home Kit

The new Arsenal 2017-2018 home shirt will introduce a stunning look, inspired by the famous 2005-06 kit.



For the first time since Puma took over from Nike, the Arsenal 2017-2018 home shirt will have a darker, "more historic" shade of red than usual. It will be not as dark as the red used for Arsenal's infamous 2005-06 Highbury goodbye jersey, but is nevertheless set to be a hit amongst supporters.



Arsenal 2017-18 Away Kit




After last season's Arsenal away jersey introduced a classic look in yellow and navy, the new Arsenal 17-18 away kit is expected to be much more modern. It will not only come with a fresh light blue and navy color combination but will also feature a blurred fusion of colors.

Arsenal 2017-2018 Third Shirt

Arsenal's new third shirt will feature the same colors as this fan tee by Puma.



The new Arsenal 17-18 third shirt is designed to "reflect the dark London nights" with a design in black and dark grey heather.

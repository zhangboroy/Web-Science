Arsenal’s Chuba Akpom Set To Join Brighton On Loan

share on:FacebookTwitter Google +Pinterest Linked In
Email
Email check failed, please try again
Cancel

Arsenal’s Chuba Akpom will spend the rest of the season on loan at Brighton.

Chuba Akpom has only featured once for Arsenal in the EFL Cup this season and manager  Arsene Wenger is reportedly open to letting him leave on loan in January.

Akpom spent last season on loan at Hull but wasn’t a regular fixture as they secured promotion back to the Premier League.

Brighton manager Chris Hughton will hope Akpom can fire his side up with the Seagulls currently top of the Sky Bet Championship.

Akpom had attracted interest from Aston Villa but his temporary move to the AMEX has been agreed and is set to be finalised today.

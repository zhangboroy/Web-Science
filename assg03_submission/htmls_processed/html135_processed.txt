Arsenal striker Chuba Akpom to go on loan to Championship leaders Brighton

gossipSimon Rice for Metro.co.ukMonday 30 Jan 2017 2:59 pm 










Chuba Akpom looks set to be sent out on loan again (Getty)
Arsenal striker Chuba Akpom will join Championship side Brighton on loan until the end of the season, according to reports.
The England Under-21 international has struggled for opportunities at the Emirates this season, with just one appearance to his name so far this term.

The 21-year-old revealed how desperate he was to play for the Gunners after that game, a 4-0 win over Nottingham Forest in the EFL Cup.
‘I think right now I’d play anywhere, I’d play in goal just to get minutes, just to be involved in the team!’ he said.
It’s not worked out though and according to Goal.com he will join the Championship leaders.

It will be just the latest loan for Akpom, with the 21-year-old helping Hull City gain promotion last season and also having spells at Nottingham Forest, Coventry and Brentford.
Brighton currently lead second place Newcastle by two points and have an eight point cushion to third place Reading as they seek promotion to the Premier League.

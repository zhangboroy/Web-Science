Mohamed Elneny: Arsenal injury scare as midfielder could face spell out with calf injury


by earnedgy in Daily Star
No Comments »






 



ARSENAL fear they could be without Mohamed Elneny for several weeks after he picked up a calf injury on international duty.
 
 



 
GETTY

 
Arsenal and Egypt star Mohamed Elneny has suffered a calf injury 


The 24-year-old midfielder is currently away with Egypt at the Africa Cup of Nations and sustained the problem following their win over Ghana last Wednesday.
He has now been ruled out of Egypt’s quarter-final clash with Morocco tonight.

Egypt officials have not revealed the extent of the injury and Arsenal are eager to learn how long their man could be out for.
Should Elneny’s injury be deemed serious enough, or his country get knocked out of the tournament, he will return to London in the coming days.
The final of the Africa Cup of Nations is next Sunday.


 
GETTY

 
Mohamed Elneny in action for Arsenal recently 




Arsenal face Watford, Chelsea and Hull in the Premier League over the coming weeks before their Champions League Round of 16 first-leg against Bayern Munich on February 15.
Arsene Wenger currently only has Santi Cazorla out injured and will be hopeful that Elneny’s issue is not serious.

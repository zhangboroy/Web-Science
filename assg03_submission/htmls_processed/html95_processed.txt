Arsene Wenger backs Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain to switch to central role after impressive Arsenal display

Chris Davie for Metro.co.ukMonday 30 Jan 2017 10:05 am 










Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain impressed in a central role against Southampton. (Getty Images)
Arsene Wenger predicts that Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain will evolve into a central midfielder following his impressive display against Southampton in the FA Cup on Saturday.
The 23-year-old was part of a midfield trio alongside Ainsley Maitland-Niles and Jeff Reine-Adelaide at St Mary’s as Wenger’s side ran out 5-0 winners over the EFL Cup finalists.
Oxlade-Chamberlain’s best position remains in doubt, having also played as a winger and as a right-back this season, but Wenger believes the England international has what it takes to move into a central role.
‘I think he’s a good flank player and a good central player,’ said Wenger.

Juve increase offer for Arsenal target


				Juventus have made an increased offer for Schalke 04 left-back Sead Kolasinac, according to reports.
Having lost the services of Patrice Evra to Marseille this month, the Serie A leaders are believed to be looking to fill the void left by the Frenchman and have targeted Schalke’s 23-year-old Bosnian.
Over a fortnight ago, reports in Italy claimed Juve offered the Bundesliga club €4million (£3.40m) which was quickly turned down. But Transfermarketweb.com now say the Italian side have tabled an increased offer of €5.5million (£4.68m).

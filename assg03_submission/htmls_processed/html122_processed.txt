Only Arsenal and Tottenham can overtake Chelsea, says Rio Ferdinand

Chris Davie for Metro.co.ukSunday 29 Jan 2017 3:37 pm 










Only Arsenal and Tottenham can catch Chelsea, says Rio Ferdinand. (AMA/Getty Images)
Rio Ferdinand believes only Arsenal and Tottenham are capable of overtaking Chelsea in this season’s title race.
Antonio Conte’s side currently lead by eight points in the Premier League, with Arsenal in second and Tottenham, who are nine points behind Chelsea, in third.

And Ferdinand believes Liverpool will be out of the title race if they fail to beat Conte’s men at Anfield on Tuesday night.
‘There are ways of getting at Chelsea. There’s space behind their wing-backs. But they have the know-how to get a result at Anfield,’ Ferdinand wrote in his column for The Sunday Times.

Chelsea are eight points clear at the top of the Premier League. (Getty Images)

‘If Liverpool don’t win, they’re out of the title race – though in reality I see Arsenal and Tottenham as the only clubs capable of overhauling Chelsea.
‘Liverpool, Manchester United and Manchester City are fighting it out below them. The title race is a matter for London. Fourth place is a battle for the northwest.’

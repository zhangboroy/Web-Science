Arsenal transfer news: Antoine Griezmann targeted as Gunners challenge Manchester United for £86million Atletico Madrid star

Spanish giants are resigned to losing the France striker this season and Gunners are ready to break the bank for him




Gossip




					By TOBY GANNON				


29th January 2017, 9:41 am

ARSENAL are set to take on Manchester United for £86million target Antoine Griezmann, according to reports.
Atletico Madrid are resigned to losing the Frenchman to United next season.




			EPA		

5



		Arsenal are set to take on Manchester United for £86million target Antoine Griezmann	

But the Gunners, who failed to lure Griezmann, 25, to the Emirates last summer, have not given up in their bid to sign him, reports the Daily Mail.


Keep up-to-date with all the latest transfer news and gossip ahead of the January window with SunSport’s daily LIVE blog


Arsenal were fully aware of the striker’s £86m release clause, a fee more than DOUBLE the £42m they paid for record signing Mesut Ozil from Real Madrid in 2013.




			Reuters		

5



		Atletico Madrid star Antoine Griezmann has scored 14 goals in all competitions this season	





			Reuters		

5



		Arsenal boss Arsene Wenger is ready to break the bank to sign Antoine Griezmann	

United are confident they can land Griezmann, who has been receiving calls EVERY day by national team-mate Paul Pogba.
It is thought the Red Devils would offer Griezmann more money than Arsenal, though the Gunners are prepared to stump up more than £250,000 a week on a player of his calibre.

Atletico’s audacious bid to sign Alexis Sanchez could become part of a swap deal.
Meanwhile, Atletico boss Diego Simeone revealed he would not attempt to block a move for star striker Griezmann, who has 14 goals in all competitions this season.
He said: “I don’t tie anybody down. I simply work in order to continue getting better for the good of the club.
Antoine Griezmann and Atletico Madrid team mates do mannequin challenge at a party




			Getty Images		

5



		Manchester United are confident they can land Antoine Griezmann, with Paul Pogba calling his France team-mate EVERY day	





			Getty Images		

5



		Atletico Madrid are resigned to losing star striker Antoine Griezmann this summer	

"He [Griezmann] is in an extraordinary moment, he is working well, he has got back on the scoresheet. He is currently in great form and it's normal that the top clubs in the world want him.
"I'm not surprised that those that can afford him are seeking him."
Griezmann is also reportedly subject to a £110m bid from cash-rich Inter Milan.
The Italian giants also want Simeone to boss the club back to Serie A glory.

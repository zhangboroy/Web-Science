Karim Benzema to Arsenal transfer news: Arsene Wenger dismisses renewed speculation of Arsenal move for Real Madrid striker Karim Benzema


by earnedgy in Evening Standard
No Comments »






 


Arsene Wenger has brushed away reports of interest in Real Madrid striker and long-term target Karim Benzema.
The Spanish giants face a challenge keeping the French striker and Alvaro Morata happy in their expensively assembled frontline and whilst neither is expected to leave in the summer Standard Sport reported that the future of both could be in doubt at the end of the season.
Arsenal have long coveted Benzema and made their most concerted attempt to sign the Frenchman during the summer of 2015 – where he turned down the chance to leave Real Madrid – but Wenger insisted that going back in for the 29-year-old was not on his mind, even in the summer.
Asked why he was so consistently linked with Benzema Wenger said: “Because he is French?

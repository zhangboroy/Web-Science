Juventus chief plays down Massimiliano Allegri link with Arsenal

Chris Davie for Metro.co.ukMonday 30 Jan 2017 12:15 pm 










Massimiliano Allegri is reportedly in advanced talks with Arsenal. (NurPhoto/Getty Images).
Juventus director Beppe Marotta has played down reports linking Massimiliano Allegri with a move to Arsenal.
The Sunday Express has claimed that the Gunners are in advanced talks with the Italian about replacing Arsene Wenger at the end of the season.

Wenger’s current contract expires this summer and the Frenchman has not yet given an indication as to whether he will extend his deal.
‘Allegri has a contract with us and I reaffirm we have an excellent rapport which has brought us great success,’ said Marotta.

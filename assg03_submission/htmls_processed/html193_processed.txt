BT pundit picks out the Arsenal star who impressed him against Southampton


				BT Sport pundit Martin Keown has sent some praise towards Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain’s way after Arsenal’s 5-0 thumping of Southampton.
Many anticipated a tough evening at the St. Mary’s stadium for the Gunners this weekend but a dominant first half display eased any concerns as the away side went 3-0 ahead by halftime.
With the likes of Mesut Ozil and Aaron Ramsey not involved in the FA Cup tie, Alex Oxlade-Chamberlain was ordered to feature in central midfield for the first time in quite a while.

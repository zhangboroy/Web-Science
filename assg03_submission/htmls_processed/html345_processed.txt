Arsenal, Manchester Utd, Manchester City discussed in Transfer Centre special podcast | Football News


by earnedgy in Sky Sports
No Comments »






 



Last Updated: 30/01/17 2:14pm













Plenty of time for deals to be done – we review which managers could be doing business on Deadline Day
On the eve of Deadline Day, Arsene Wenger reveals why he hasn’t signed a player this window – but does he have a strong enough squad to compete for the title?


 

                     Listen: Transfer Centre special podcast                
                    Which managers will be doing business on Deadline Day?                



Manchester United are through to the last 16 of the FA Cup, but could one of their players be about to leave? Ashley Young has been linked with a move to China – find out what the Portuguese had to say about players making the switch to Asia.
Former West Ham striker Tony Cottee joined us to discuss the sale of Dimitri Payet, the possible signing of Brentford’s Scott Hogan and the possibility of bringing Joe Hart back to the Premier League. We also hear from Tony Pulis and Sam Allardyce after Crystal Palace completed the signing of Patrick Van Aanholt.
Don’t forget Sky Sports News HQ is the place to be for all the transfer news up until and including Deadline Day. We’ll keep you across every deal as it happens from 6am tomorrow.

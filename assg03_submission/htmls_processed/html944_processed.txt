Antoine Griezmann to Man United: Atletico open talks with Arsenal star to replace striker
ATLETICO MADRID are ready to make Arsenal star Alexis Sanchez their star man next season with Antoine Griezmann expected to leave for Manchester United, according to reports.

                                        Dan Gibbs

January 30, 2017 Leave a Comment 
Kadib orintii taageerayaasha R. Madrid Arsenal oo jaanis loo siiyay saxiixa BenzemaKadib orintii xalay ay ku oriyeen xidiga weerarka ugu ciyaara Real Madrid Karim Benzema, ayaa waxaa soo baxaya warar sheegaya, in xidigaan loo bandhigay saxiixiisa kooxaha Arsenal iyo Chelsea.



Madaxweynaha Real Madrid Florentino Perez ayaa la fahansan yahay in uusan la dhacsaneyn 29 jirka, gaar ahaan kaddib markii ay ku ooriyeen jamaahiirta.
Perez waxa uu u sheegay dhinacyada Premier League-ga in uu diyaar u yahay iibinta faransiiska.
